⚠ GPIO 26–37 DO NOT USE — ESP32-S3 WROOM-1 internally connects these to SPI Flash.
⚠ ADC2 (GPIO 11–20) — Unreliable when WiFi is ON. All sensors on ADC1 (GPIO 1–10).
